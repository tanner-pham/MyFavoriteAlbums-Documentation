# Artists' Albums
*(Updated February 19, 2026)*

This guide explains how to use the **Artists' Albums** tab to view one artist’s entries other quick summary metrics.

> **Note**: This tab requires clicking **Submit** to show updated table and summary values.

## Get Started

Before using this tab, confirm the following:

- The app is running.
- You can open **Artists' Albums** from the top tab row.
- The artist dropdown and **Submit** button are visible.

## How to Use Artists' Albums

1. Open **Artists' Albums**.
2. Choose a band or artist from the dropdown.
3. Click **Submit**.
4. Review the table and summary values below it.

![Artists' Albums tab showing artist dropdown and Submit button before results are loaded.](../Resources/Screenshot%202026-02-20%20at%2012.54.21 AM.png)

## Page Response

After you click **Submit**, this tab shows:

- A table of albums for the selected artist' album, and respective year and rating.
- Underneath the table, a count of how many albums are ranked for that artist.
- An average rating summary value.

![Artists' Albums tab showing populated album table plus album count and average rating summary text.](../Resources/Screenshot%202026-02-20%20at%2012.51.07 AM.png)

## Troubleshooting

`I changed artists but the results stayed the same`
- Click **Submit** to refresh outputs.

`I cannot find an artist in the dropdown`
- Confirm the artist name exists in your data file.
- Restart the app after editing data.

`The average rating or album count is missing`
- Re-select the artist and click **Submit** again.
- Confirm the selected artist has at least one row in the data.
